Wash hands with soap and water after touching animals and animal products.

When coughing or sneezing cover mouth and nose with flexed elbow or tissue.

If you are coughing or sneezing, wear a mask and avoid contact with other employee's.

Avoid close contact with animals that are sick.

Ensure all food you eat is well cooked.

Avoid travel if you have a fever or cough.

If you have a fever, cough &/or difficulty breathing seek medical care as soon as possible.
